# Trust over IP Foundation (ToIP) Annual Review – 2025

## Overview

The Trust over IP Foundation (ToIP) continued to expand its role in decentralized digital trust infrastructure during 2025 through standards development, ecosystem collaboration, governance innovation, and emerging work in AI trust and decentralized trust relationships.

Major focus areas during the year included:
- AI and Human Trust,
- decentralized trust graphs,
- KERI ecosystem specifications,
- governance and interoperability frameworks,
- trust registries and TSP-related work,
- educational outreach,
- and cross-ecosystem collaboration with organizations such as DIF, GLEIF, ISO/IEC, and LF Decentralized Trust.

2025 also marked planning for the ToIP 5th Anniversary Showcase and Symposium.

---

# Key Working Group Activities

## Technology Stack Working Group

The TSWG continued work related to:
- Trust Spanning Protocol (TSP),
- trust registries,
- interoperability discussions,
- governance alignment,
- and technical architecture evolution.

## Ecosystem & Governance Working Group

The EGWG supported:
- governance discussions,
- ecosystem coordination,
- educational outreach,
- symposium planning,
- and the “Bite-Sized Trust” educational initiative.

## KERI Suite Working Group

The KSWG advanced work on:
- KERI,
- ACDC,
- CESR,
- and related cryptographic trust infrastructure.

A major milestone was approval of the SAID URN Task Force focused on IANA registration of SAID identifiers.

The Steering Committee also worked extensively on improving publication and specification approval processes.

## AI & Human Trust Working Group

A major achievement in 2025 was formal approval and launch of the AI & Human Trust Working Group (AIM WG).

The AIM WG focuses on:
- trustworthy AI systems,
- AI agent trust,
- provenance and authenticity,
- delegation and accountability,
- and applying ToIP architecture to AI ecosystems.

## Decentralized Trust Graph Working Group

Another major milestone was launch of the Decentralized Trust Graph Working Group (DTGWG).

The DTGWG focuses on decentralized trust relationships using:
- DIDs,
- verifiable credentials,
- privacy-preserving proofs,
- trust registries,
- and governance frameworks.

The group launched in September 2025 and rapidly established international participation and multiple task forces.
